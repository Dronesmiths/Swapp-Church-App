Groups built for ministry momentum

Whether you are organizing growth track cohorts or compassion teams, SWAPP keeps people, communication, and next steps aligned in one workspace.

  • Centralize all ministry groups with clear categories, tags, and visibility controls.
  • Help members discover and join groups with filters for campus, life stage, or meeting rhythm.
  • Empower leaders with shared files, automated reminders, and attendance tracking.

Designed with leaders in mind

Group dashboards surface new join requests, upcoming events, and prayer activity so shepherds know what to prioritize each week.

Notes, milestones, and care escalations sync to pastoral reports, keeping staff and lay leaders unified.

What members experience

Joining a ministry team or discipleship circle takes just a few taps, and every group offers its own rhythm for connection and care.

  • Browse featured ministries, search by need, and request to join instantly.
  • Engage in private prayer feeds that keep sensitive updates within the group.
  • RSVP to gatherings, serve days, or trainings synchronized with their calendars.
  • Message leaders or the whole group with announcements, reminders, and encouragement.

Everything in one stream

Members see prayer updates, event RSVPs, and shared resources in a single feed tailored to their ministry involvement.

Leaders can pin highlights, celebrate answered prayers, and track pastoral follow-ups without leaving the app.

Leader workflow highlights
