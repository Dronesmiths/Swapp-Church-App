Why it matters

Teams fueled by Buildingit LLC's outreach partners needed one shared space to plan, pray, and measure neighborhood ministry. Outreach Maps unite volunteers, pastors, and data so leaders see the gospel moving street by street.

Built with ministry impact in mind

From the Every Neighborhood Network to regional Serve Day coalitions, outreach organizations rely on consistent territory visibility. SWAPP keeps every leader aligned with folders for campuses, permissions for teams, and on-the-go updates.

Key capabilities

Equip your compassion, evangelism, and follow-up teams with tools that work in the field and at the office.

  • Design digital territories with color-coded boundaries and filters by status or ministry lane.
  • Check volunteers in/out from any device and auto-log time served for reporting.
  • Group locations into folders for campuses, neighborhoods, and missional assignments.
  • Track progress with real-time visit outcomes, next steps, and goal dashboards.
  • Switch between map and list views to plan routes, print sheets, or power mobile checklists.

Made for leaders and volunteers

Volunteers can tap to check in, see assignments, and add notes while area leaders monitor which territories are covered and where support is needed. Admins manage permissions, folders, and templates once—every campus inherits the plan instantly.

Integrations with guest follow-up and prayer flows ensure every visit outcome launches the right care journey automatically.

Field-tested ministry impact

Partner churches serving with Compassion Coalition teams reported 37% faster follow-up because territory folders surfaced households still awaiting care. Outreach Maps kept door-to-door crews synced with central prayer rooms and benevolence teams.
