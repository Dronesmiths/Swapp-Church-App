Church-wide & ministry-specific feeds

Build a central prayer home for your entire congregation while equipping individual ministries with their own spaces to rally prayer teams.

  • Organize requests by campus, ministry, or language with clear visibility for the whole staff.
  • Allow members to subscribe to the feeds that matter most so they can respond quickly.
  • Surface answered prayers and celebrate testimonies in Sunday services or newsletters.

Context without complexity

Each request carries contact details, care status, and notes from your team so every follow-up feels personal. Automatic reminders keep long-term needs in front of intercessors and pastors.

Instant notifications that mobilize prayer

Push notifications, SMS, and email alerts make sure urgent needs are covered within minutes. Teams can tailor the cadence so members are informed without feeling overwhelmed.

  • Choose announcement templates for emergencies, praise reports, and weekly roundups.
  • Highlight action buttons so volunteers can call, visit, or deliver meals with one tap.
  • Track engagement to see how many members opened, prayed, or shared the request.

Care teams stay coordinated

Assign owners to each request and receive instant alerts when updates are posted. No more wondering if someone followed up—activity logs show every prayer, comment, and check-in.

Admin moderation built for shepherds

Protect sensitive details while keeping prayer easy to share. Moderators can review, anonymize, or route requests to the right leaders before anything goes live.

  • Set visibility rules to ensure pastoral staff sees every confidential request first.
  • Empower trusted volunteers to approve posts while maintaining accountability.
  • Use smart filters to catch keywords that may need extra care or escalation.

Pastoral impact you can measure

Dashboards highlight households engaged in prayer, people still awaiting a response, and stories ready to celebrate. Pastors gain a real-time view of spiritual needs so sermons, visits, and small group curricula can respond with compassion.
