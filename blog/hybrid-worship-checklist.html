Hybrid worship checklist for production teams

Align broadcast, in-room cues, and post-service follow-up so every worship moment engages on-site and online attenders.

January 30, 2024 • 9 min read

Hybrid worship thrives on coordination. This checklist walks through pre-service tech checks, volunteer huddles, and real-time communication tools inside SWAPP to keep everyone aligned.

Build your service plan with scenes and cues so each transition, lyric slide, and video handoff is shared with both in-room and broadcast teams. Add contingency notes for volunteer leaders so they can troubleshoot quickly.

After service, trigger automated follow-up for digital guests, send recap clips to your communications team, and log wins for next week's planning huddle.
