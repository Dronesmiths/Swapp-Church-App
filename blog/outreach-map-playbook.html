Guest follow-up that lasts beyond Sunday

The Outreach Map Playbook helps your team log every home visit, assign the right volunteer, and report back on life change in minutes.

May 28, 2024 • 7 min read

Churches using SWAPP wanted one space to track guest follow-up from a first visit through group connection. The Outreach Map Playbook gives campuses a shared digital map so leaders can prioritize neighborhoods, assign volunteers, and celebrate stories in real time.

Start by importing recent guest households and categorizing them by preferred follow-up channel. From there, volunteer captains can drag-and-drop assignments, leave notes after each visit, and trigger automated reminders for pastors to make a personal connection.

How teams stay coordinated

The map view displays color-coded pins for each stage of your guest journey. When a volunteer checks in at a home, SWAPP updates the record instantly, notifying small group leaders and pastoral care. The result: no family slips through the cracks, and your Sunday welcome flows naturally into weekday care.

Pair the map with the built-in follow-up queues to see exactly who needs prayer, a phone call, or a coffee invite. Templates keep communication consistent, while analytics surface which neighborhoods are most responsive.

What to do next

Ready to tailor the playbook for your ministry? Our onboarding team will help you import data, design volunteer tags, and craft the next-step nudges that match your church culture. With SWAPP, your care team can confidently reach every guest beyond Sunday.
