Designing a generosity moment every week

Use giving analytics, story prompts, and follow-up cues to help your church celebrate generosity with clarity and joy.

March 11, 2024 • 5 min read

The best generosity moments feel pastoral, celebratory, and clear about next steps. SWAPP giving analytics surface timely stories so your hosts can confidently communicate impact and invite recurring participation.

Begin by reviewing the Giving Pulse each Monday. Identify notable trends—first-time givers, reactivated households, or campaign milestones—and craft a short story to share on the weekend.

Frame the moment

Use the built-in prompt library to pair scripture, gratitude language, and ministry outcomes. Add a quick video testimony or photo from the field and schedule it into your service rundown with the SWAPP media timeline.

During the moment, direct the congregation to the app for easy giving options, mission updates, and follow-up resources. After service, automated thank-you messages reinforce the invitation and spotlight the next initiative.

Measure what matters

Track recurring giving growth, weekend participation, and engagement with impact stories. When you see spikes, follow up with personal appreciation to build deeper partnership with your donors.
