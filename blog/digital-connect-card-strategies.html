Digital connect card strategies that spark next steps

Combine automation, human touch, and team dashboards to move every new guest toward discipleship pathways that fit their story.

December 12, 2023 • 6 min read

SWAPP digital connect cards capture the right details without slowing down your welcome moments. Customize the form for each campus, gather preferred next steps, and add optional prompts for prayer or serving interests.

As submissions roll in, SWAPP routes each response to the right ministry lane. Pastoral care sees prayer needs, group leaders get notified about connection requests, and your guest services team receives reminders to send a handwritten note.

Pair the workflow with the Guest Journey dashboard so staff can celebrate milestones—salvation decisions, baptisms, group sign-ups—and identify gaps in your follow-up process.
