App requirements

What devices does the SWAPP Church App support?

The app is delivered as branded iOS, Android, and web experiences, ensuring members can participate from any modern smartphone, tablet, or browser.

Do we need technical staff to manage the platform?

No specialized IT team is required. SWAPP provides a guided onboarding, automation templates, and an intuitive admin console so ministry leaders can make updates without coding.

Is there a minimum size for our church?

SWAPP is optimized for congregations of any size—from launch teams to multi-campus ministries—with pricing tiers that scale seats, groups, and outreach tools as you grow.

How secure is member data?

All member interactions, giving records, and messages are encrypted in transit and at rest, with role-based permissions limiting access to sensitive information.

Admin dashboard availability

Who gets admin dashboard access?

You can assign admin roles to pastors, ministry leads, or communications staff. Each role unlocks only the tools they need, from content scheduling to reporting.

Can we monitor engagement in real time?

Yes. The dashboard includes live analytics for app usage, giving, and group interactions so you can spot trends and respond immediately.

Is there training for new admins?

Every plan includes a launch coach, weekly office hours, and a knowledge base with step-by-step tutorials to help new team members ramp quickly.

Does the dashboard integrate with other systems?

SWAPP supports data exports and integrations with leading church management software, making it easy to sync attendance, giving, and volunteer lists.

Ministry prayer feeds

How do prayer requests appear in the app?

Members can submit requests directly in the app, and approved updates post to dedicated prayer feeds for each ministry, campus, or small group.

Can prayer feeds be moderated?

Absolutely. Admins can require approval before publishing, assign moderators, and set guidelines to ensure every update aligns with church standards.

Will members get notified about new requests?

Push notifications and email digests automatically notify followers when new prayer needs or praise reports are posted, keeping the congregation engaged.

Can we archive answered prayers?

Yes, answered requests can be archived or celebrated with stories that remain accessible for testimony highlights in sermons and newsletters.

Registration steps

What information do we need to register?

Be ready with your church name, primary contact, estimated congregation size, and branding assets so we can configure your app instantly.

How long does approval take?

Most churches receive confirmation within two business days, followed by a kickoff call to finalize launch milestones.

When can members download the app?

Once your branding is approved, SWAPP publishes to the App Store, Google Play, and web hub—typically within three weeks of registration.

Are there setup fees?

Setup is included in every plan. Optional custom development or data migrations are quoted separately if you need bespoke workflows.

Additional questions

What type of support is available after launch?

All tiers include email support with same-day response and access to a resource library. Standard and Plus plans add live chat, while Plus offers a dedicated success manager.

How do billing and contracts work?

Plans are billed annually with price protection for the contract term. Multi-year agreements qualify for discounted rates and onboarding perks.

Can SWAPP help with content strategy?

Yes, ministry coaches share templates for sermon notes, events, giving campaigns, and volunteer onboarding to keep your content calendar full.

Do you offer multilingual support?

The app interface supports English and Spanish at launch, with additional languages available through custom translation services.
